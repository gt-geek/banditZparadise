BanditZParadise – Server Rules

Community Rules

  • Treat all players with respect at all times.
  • Political and religious discussions are not allowed.
  • Hate speech of any kind is strictly forbidden.
  • Use Discord channels only for their intended purpose.
  • NSFW content is not permitted.
  • Spamming is prohibited.
  • The admin team has the final decision in all situations.

General Rules

  • English is required in-game and on Discord.
  • Private Steam profiles are not allowed.
  • Combat logging is forbidden and will result in a wiped inventory.
  • You must either move 1000 meters away or wait 10 minutes before logging out.
  • Exploiting bugs or glitches is not allowed.
  • Cheating results in a permanent ban.
  • NVIDIA Inspector usage is considered cheating.
  • Stream sniping is prohibited.
  • Metagaming about base locations or player positions is not allowed (general fight callouts are fine).
  • Disrespecting staff (in-game or Discord) leads to a ban.
  • Impersonating staff members is prohibited.
  • Racism, homophobia, toxicity, or any hate speech will not be tolerated.
  • Do not DM or tag admins — use the ticket system.
  • No vehicle compensation.
  • No compensation without valid video proof.
  • No compensation for thrown items.
  • If you accidentally log into someone else's base, you must not raid it and must F11 immediately.
  • Real-money trading (RMT) is strictly forbidden.
  • Logout limits (automatically enforced):
    • 0 breaching charges (any type)
    • 0 gunpowder, sulfur, or charcoal powder
    • Max 3 claymores
    • Max 10 grenades (M7, EGD-5, 40mm explosive)

Faction Rules

  • Group limits:
    • Max 6 online players, up to 6 offline members
    • Exceeding limits may result in admin action.
  • Alt accounts must remain in the same group as the main account.
  • Alliances or teaming between groups are not allowed.
  • Trading or visiting other bases during raid time is prohibited.
  • Once you leave a group, you cannot rejoin it.

Base Building Rules

  • Every base must have at least one accessible raid entrance.
  • Bases must be reachable on foot.
  • Clipping gates into objects (static or player-built) is not allowed.
  • Gates inside raid tunnels must be fully accessible.
  • Only one base (or vanilla structure) per group.
  • Maximum of 2 flagpoles per base.
  • For unclear build areas, open a support ticket.
  • Max 20 codelocked gates per base (excess will be removed).
  • No building within 200 meters of military zones.
  • Building in police stations, hospitals, prisons, or guardhouses is forbidden.
  • Floating bases are not allowed.
  • Building on roads is prohibited.
  • Barbed wire tunnels are not allowed.
  • Ladders may not be used as traps.
  • Log walls are prohibited.
  • Blocking access to storage or gate frames is not allowed.
  • Garden plots may not be used for base reinforcement.
  • Walls and watchtowers are limited to 6 levels in height.

Raiding Rules

  • Raid begins when gates are destroyed or a base is forcefully entered.
  • Raid remains active for 60 minutes after explosives are used or gates are attacked.
  • During a raid, only previously destroyed gates may be rebuilt and locked.
  • Defenders may rebuild fully when: no damage occurred for 60 minutes AND all raiders have left.
  • Raid schedule: Friday 16:00 – Sunday 23:59 (Berlin time), gate raiding only.
  • Raids must be fully recorded (entry to exit). Failure to provide recording = 7-day ban.

Additional Rules

  • Using anything other than homemade/heavy breaching charges is at your own risk.
  • No compensation for explosives or ammo.
  • Blocking base entrances is prohibited.
  • Destroying or despawning loot intentionally is not allowed (vehicles excluded).
  • Logging out inside enemy bases is forbidden.
  • Taking over bases is not allowed.
  • Glitching through walls is prohibited (windows allowed).
  • Boosting rules:
    • Max 2 objects total (players or items combined)
    • Example: 1 player + 1 object OR 2 objects OR 2 players
    • Extra allowance: 1 additional player or item during raids
    • Excluded items: car parts, trucks, garden plots, fireplaces, MMG items
  • Loot handling:
    • Loot must be stored properly after a raid.
    • If storage is removed, loot must be transferred.
    • Logging out during a raid counts as combat logging.
    • If kicked or disconnected, you must rejoin immediately.
